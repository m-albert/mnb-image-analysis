# Image Analysis Course

Welcome to the website of the Image Analysis course offered by the Microscopy Network Basel (MNB).

The course introduces the fundamentals of (bio-)image analysis and focuses on Python-based workflows for its practical sessions.

## Date & Location

The course will take place on March 17, 2026 from 9:00 to 17:00 at the D-BSSE, seminar room E23.
